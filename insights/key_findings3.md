## Revenue Impact Analysis

Overall Revenue Loss from Failed Transactions

Total processed transaction volume:

€7,107,739

Revenue lost due to failed transactions:

€669,733

Share of revenue lost:

9.42%

Interpretation:

Approximately one in every ten euros processed fails, indicating a significant operational and financial impact. Even small improvements in payment success rates could lead to meaningful revenue recovery.

## Revenue Loss by Payment Processing Stage

Stage	Failed Revenue	Share
Authorization	€362,380	54.11%
Capture	€190,754	28.48%
Settlement	€116,598	17.41%

Insight:

More than half of the lost revenue occurs during the authorization stage, confirming that the main issues happen before the transaction is fully processed.

This suggests problems related to:

issuer declines

risk validation

payment verification

processor communication

Improving authorization success rates would have the largest financial impact.

## Revenue Loss by Payment Processor

Processor	Failed Revenue	Failure Rate
processor_A	€298,373	8.41%
processor_B	€251,680	11.94%
processor_C	€119,680	8.23%

Key insight:

Although processor_A generates the highest absolute failed revenue due to higher transaction volume, processor_B demonstrates the worst reliability with the highest failure rate.

This indicates that processor_B may require performance monitoring or routing optimization.
