1. Merchant Segment Analysis
### Merchant Segment Impact

- Enterprise merchants show the highest failure rate (10.21%).
- SMB segment has the largest transaction volume and contributes the majority of failed transactions.
- Despite similar failure rates across segments, SMB represents the biggest operational impact due to scale.

2. Repeat vs First-time Customers
### Customer Behavior

- First-time customers have a slightly higher failure rate (9.48%) compared to repeat customers (9.33%).
- The difference is small, suggesting that failures are more related to payment infrastructure than customer familiarity.

3. IMPORTANT - Bad Combo Analysis
### High-Risk Payment Patterns

- Card transactions processed via processor_B show frequent failures during capture stage.
- Authorization stage appears repeatedly across high-failure combinations.
- This indicates potential processor-specific instability rather than customer-related issues.
